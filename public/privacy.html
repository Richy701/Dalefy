Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Information We Collect

When you use Dalefy, we collect information you provide directly:

  • Your name and profile information
  • Trip details, itineraries, and events you create
  • Photos and videos you upload to trip galleries
  • Device information for push notifications and app functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve Dalefy's trip planning features, sync your data across devices, and deliver notifications about your trips. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Cloud Storage). Photos and files are stored in Firebase Storage. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections.

Sharing

Trip information is shared only with other members of the same trip. When you share a trip link, the recipient can view the published itinerary. You control what is shared through the app's sharing features.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@dalefy.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@dalefy.com.
