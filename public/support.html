FAQ

How do I join a trip?
Ask your trip organizer for the trip PIN, then enter it on the Join screen in the app.

Can I upload photos?
Yes. Go to the Gallery tab and tap the + button to upload photos from your camera roll.

How do I see the itinerary?
Open your trip and tap Schedule to see the day-by-day itinerary your organizer has planned.
